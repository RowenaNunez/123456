OUR BEST SELLERS PRODUCTS

Bibingka

Bibingka is a soft, baked Filipino rice cake made with coconut milk, often topped with salted egg or cheese, and traditionally served warm in banana leaves.

Suman

Suman is a Filipino sticky rice cake made with glutinous rice and coconut milk, wrapped in leaves, and steamed. It's mildly sweet and often served with sugar or latik.

Puto

Puto is a soft, steamed Filipino rice cake, mildly sweet, often topped with cheese or salted egg, and served as a snack or side dish.
